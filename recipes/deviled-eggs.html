Deviled Eggs

Description

This is an easy to follow recipe to make some deviled eggs with a little bit of texture from the onions and the celery.

Recipe credit goes to allrecipes.com.

Ingredients

  • 6 hard-boiled eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon prepared mustard
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped celery
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch paprika, or to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Peel hard-boiled eggs
  2. Slice eggs in half lengthwise and remove yolks; set whites aside.
  3. Mash yolks with a for in a small bowl. Stir mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, onion, and celery; mix well and season with salt to taste.
  4. Stuff or pipe yolk mixture into egg whites.
  5. Sprinkle with paprika. Refrigerate eggs until serving.
