Instant Ramen, but better!

Description

There will be a description of how this is a recipe of instant ramen, with some modifications added, such as bacon bits and other carbonara ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of ramen (One cup per packet of ramen)
  • Two packets of instant ramen
  • Bacon Bits

Steps

  1. Bring your water to boil in a sauce pan.
  2. Add ramen to boiling water, side by side.
  3. Profit.
